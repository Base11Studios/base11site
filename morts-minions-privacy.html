--- title: Morts Minions Privacy Policy heading: Morts Minions Privacy Policy subtitle: Your progress stays with you. ---

Morts Minions stores your player progress data locally on your device and, if enabled, backs it up to your personal iCloud account so that you can pick up where you left off across devices.

Base11Studios does not collect, transmit, or have access to any gameplay data stored on your device or in iCloud. Everything stays tied to your Apple ID.

The app does not gather personal information beyond what is required for the core gameplay experience. You control your data through your device settings and iCloud preferences.
